We are here to to help


Find clear FAQs online and customer service available at the end of the phone seven days a week.


Do I need to confirm in advance if travelling on a different train?

You don't need to let us know if you want to change trains, but you will need to check the terms of your tickets to see if it'll still be valid for a different train

How much luggage can i take on the train?

You are allowed to carry 1 personal bag(laptop bag/purse), 1 seven kg bag and 1 more bag of 23kg.

How will I receive my ticket?

You can download your ticket from your account or you can select send mail option where we will email your ticket.

What do the different seat option mean ?

Different seat options are:

Forward Facing
You will be facing the front of the train.
Backward Facing
You will be facing back of the train.
Airline style
Type of seat that's not around a table.
